Christine Tantiwa Kinson established her London based menswear line in 2015, after graduating from the prestigious Royal College of Art with a Master’s degree in Fashion Menswear. With a strong focus on technical outerwear, Wang’s garments range from the relatively minimal to the boldly conceptual and wholly futuristic, always with a forward-thinking functionality.

Each collection is inspired by Wang’s life experiences and social interactions, tackling subject matter that is both deeply intimate and widely relatable.

In her Autumn/Winter collection for the 2017/18 season, inspiration comes from the world as experienced through the senses. The body remains a point of focus through flesh-tones and skin-like textures. Fabric covers and interacts with the human form in ruching and rolls, gathering to form cocoons that reflecting a fundamental human need: to feel safe.
